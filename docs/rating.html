Rating View Customizations

Overview

This documentation covers the customizations made to the Rating view in Odoo. The main enhancement is the addition of a priority field to the rating tree view, allowing users to prioritize and manage ratings more effectively.

Key Customizations

  • Addition of a priority field to the rating tree view.
  • Use of the priority widget to visually represent rating priorities.

Major Features

  • Ability to prioritize ratings for better management and follow-up.
  • Visual representation of priority levels in the rating list.

Use Case Scenarios

  • Support teams can quickly identify high-priority ratings that require immediate attention.
  • Managers can filter and sort ratings based on priority to allocate resources effectively.

Technical Implementation

  • XML inheritance of the standard rating tree view (rating.rating_rating_view_tree).
  • Insertion of the priority field after the res_name field using xpath.
  • Use of the priority widget for visual representation.

Benefits

  • Improved rating management and prioritization.
  • Enhanced visibility of high-priority ratings for support and management teams.
  • Streamlined workflow for handling customer feedback and ratings.
