CRM View Customizations

Overview

This documentation covers the customizations made to the CRM views in Odoo. Enhancements include additional fields for leads, new menu items for CRM configuration, and improved access to marketing campaign data.

Key Customizations

  • Adds the email_from field to the CRM lead form for better tracking of lead sources.
  • Introduces a new "Crm Lists" menu under CRM configuration for easier navigation.
  • Adds menu items for Campaigns, Mediums, and Sources, linking to their respective actions and views.
  • Defines a custom action for viewing UTM Sources in a list view.

Major Features

  • Enhanced lead management with direct access to the lead's email address.
  • Centralized configuration for CRM marketing elements (Campaigns, Mediums, Sources).
  • Quick access to UTM source data for marketing analysis.

Use Case Scenarios

  • Sales and marketing teams can track the origin of leads more effectively using the email_from field.
  • CRM administrators can easily configure and manage marketing campaigns, mediums, and sources from a single menu.
  • Managers can analyze UTM source data to optimize marketing strategies.

Technical Implementation

  • XML inheritance of the standard CRM lead form view (crm.crm_lead_view_form).
  • Insertion of the email_from field into the lead form using xpath.
  • Definition of new menu items and actions for CRM configuration and marketing data.

Benefits

  • Improved lead tracking and marketing attribution.
  • Streamlined CRM configuration and navigation.
  • Better data-driven decision making for marketing and sales teams.
