Sale Portal View Customizations

Overview

This documentation covers the customizations made to the Sale Portal view in Odoo. The main enhancement is the removal of the communication thread section from the sale order portal page, streamlining the customer experience and focusing on essential order details.

Key Customizations

  • Removes the communication thread (sale_order_communication div) from the sale order portal template.
  • Simplifies the portal interface for customers by hiding internal discussions and chatter.

Major Features

  • Cleaner and more focused sale order portal page for customers.
  • Prevents customers from viewing internal communications or chatter related to their orders.

Use Case Scenarios

  • Businesses that want to keep internal discussions private and not visible to customers on the portal.
  • Customers benefit from a simplified portal view, focusing only on relevant order information.

Technical Implementation

  • QWeb template inheritance of sale.sale_order_portal_template.
  • Use of xpath to replace (remove) the sale_order_communication div from the portal page.

Benefits

  • Enhanced privacy for internal communications.
  • Improved customer experience with a cleaner portal interface.
  • Reduced confusion by hiding unnecessary information from customers.
