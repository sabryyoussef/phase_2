Documents View Customizations

Overview

This documentation covers the customizations made to the Documents views in Odoo. These enhancements provide advanced document management features, improved sharing workflows, and better integration with partners, projects, and folders.

Key Customizations

  • Enhanced Document Form View with additional fields: type_id, issue_date, expiration_date, project_id, and improved partner selection.
  • Restricted quick creation and editing for key fields (e.g., type_id, folder_id, owner_id, partner_id).
  • Advanced Share Document form with options for sharing, scheduling activities, and sending emails.
  • Notebook pages for managing related documents and handover processes.
  • Improved domain and context for partner and project selection.

Major Features

  • Comprehensive document metadata management (type, issue/expiration dates, project, folder, owner, partner).
  • Controlled sharing and collaboration with advanced sharing forms and activity scheduling.
  • Notebook interface for organizing documents and handover records.
  • Enhanced security and data integrity by restricting quick creation/editing of related records.

Use Case Scenarios

  • Organizations can manage all document details, track expiration, and link documents to projects and partners.
  • Users can securely share documents, schedule follow-up activities, and send notifications directly from the document interface.
  • Administrators can ensure data consistency and prevent unauthorized creation of related records.

Technical Implementation

  • XML inheritance of standard document views and forms.
  • Use of xpath to add, move, and restrict fields and attributes.
  • Custom notebook pages and advanced form layouts for document and handover management.

Benefits

  • Improved document management and compliance tracking.
  • Streamlined sharing and collaboration workflows.
  • Greater control over document-related data and relationships.
