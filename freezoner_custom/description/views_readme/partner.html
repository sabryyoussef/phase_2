Partner View Customizations

Overview

This documentation covers the customizations made to the Partner (res.partner) form view in Odoo. The main enhancement is the addition of a new "Products" tab, allowing users to associate products with partners and manage related information efficiently.

Key Customizations

  • Addition of a new Products tab to the partner form view.
  • Display and manage project_product_ids related to the partner.
  • Support for listing project, product, date range, and remarks (future extensibility).

Major Features

  • Centralized management of products associated with each partner.
  • Easy access to product-related information from the partner form.
  • Potential for tracking project-product relationships and remarks.

Use Case Scenarios

  • Sales and account managers can quickly view and manage products linked to a partner.
  • Project managers can track which products are associated with which partners for specific projects.
  • Support and service teams can reference product associations for better customer service.

Technical Implementation

  • XML inheritance of the standard partner form view (base.view_partner_form).
  • Insertion of a new page/tab after the "Internal Notes" tab using xpath.
  • Display of project_product_ids in a list view within the new tab.
  • Commented fields for future extensibility (project, product, date range, remarks).

Benefits

  • Improved partner management and visibility of product associations.
  • Streamlined workflow for sales, project, and support teams.
  • Foundation for further customization and integration with other modules.
