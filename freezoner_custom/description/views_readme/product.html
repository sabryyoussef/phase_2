Product View Customizations

Overview

This documentation covers the customizations made to the Product views in Odoo. The enhancements include new tabs for document management, partner-related fields, and task tracking, as well as additional fields for responsible users and payment options.

Key Customizations

  • Addition of Deliverable Document and Required Document tabs to the product template form.
  • New Partner Related Fields tab for associating partner-specific fields with products.
  • New Tasks tab (visible when service_tracking is new_workflow).
  • Field responsible_id added after the barcode field for both product template and product forms.
  • Field stripe_visa and its label added after the sale_ok label for payment options.

Major Features

  • Centralized document management for each product.
  • Ability to track required and deliverable documents per product.
  • Partner-specific field management for products.
  • Task tracking for service products with new workflow.
  • Assignment of responsible users for products.
  • Support for Stripe Visa payment option.

Use Case Scenarios

  • Product managers can manage and track all required and deliverable documents for each product.
  • Sales and support teams can view and manage partner-specific product fields.
  • Service teams can track tasks related to service products with new workflow.
  • Finance teams can assign responsible users and manage payment options for products.

Technical Implementation

  • XML inheritance of standard product template and product forms.
  • Insertion of new tabs and fields using xpath expressions.
  • Conditional visibility for the Tasks tab based on service_tracking value.
  • Support for editable lists within new tabs for document and partner field management.

Benefits

  • Improved product management and compliance tracking.
  • Enhanced collaboration between product, sales, and support teams.
  • Streamlined workflow for service products and document management.
  • Greater flexibility in assigning responsibilities and payment options.
